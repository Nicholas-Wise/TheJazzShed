The Jazz Shed Home Page






Welcome to the home page of The Jazz Shed. In this website, you will find all the lessons and resources you need to get started on your jazz improvisation journey! All of our content is free. Start by browsing all of our educational content ranging from music theory concepts, to full jazz transcriptions.





Who is this website for?









This website is for beginners who are interested in learning jazz improvisation. We believe that jazz improvisation could be made easier through online tutorials and resources like our website. If you, or someone you know is interested in learning jazz improv, tell them about this website!
